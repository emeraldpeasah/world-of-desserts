    Gooey Fudgy Brownies

    Ingredients

    • 225g of quality chocolate
    • 170g unsalted butter
    • 250g sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
    • 95g plain flour
    • 30g cocoa powder

    Method

    1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line an 8-inch square baking dish with parchment paper.

    2. Chop the chocolate into chunks. Melt half of the chocolate in the microwave in 20-second intervals, saving the other half for later.

    3. In a large bowl, mix the butter and sugar with an electric hand mixer, then beat in the eggs and vanilla until the mixture becomes fluffy and light in colour.

    4. Whisk in the melted chocolate (make sure it's not too hot or else the eggs will cook), then sift in the flour and cocoa powder. Fold to incorporate the dry ingredient.

    5. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then transfer the batter to the prepared baking dish.

    6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, depending on how fudgy you like your brownies, then cool completely.

    7. Enjoy!

    Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

    Ingredients

    • 100g of granulated sugar
    • 165g brown sugar
    • 115g unsalted butter,melted
    • 1 egg
    • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
    • 155g plain flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
    • 110g milk chocolate chunks
    • 110g chocolate of your preference (white/dark)

    Method

    1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar and butter until a smooth paste.

    2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short while before falling back into the mixture.

    3. Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula

    4. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough for at least 30 minutes.

    5. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

    6. Scoop the dough with an ice-cream scoop onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving gaps between each one.

    7. Enjoy!
