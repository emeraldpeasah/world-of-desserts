    Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

    Ingredients

    • 100g of granulated sugar
    • 165g brown sugar
    • 115g unsalted butter,melted
    • 1 egg
    • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
    • 155g plain flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
    • 110g milk chocolate chunks
    • 110g chocolate of your preference (white/dark)

    Method

    1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar and butter until a smooth paste.

    2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short while before falling back into the mixture.

    3. Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula

    4. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough for at least 30 minutes.

    5. Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

    6. Scoop the dough with an ice-cream scoop onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving gaps between each one.

    7. Enjoy!
